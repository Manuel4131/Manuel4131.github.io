About me

    My name is Alston Chang and have lived in Taipei for more than 20 years. After getting my master degree, I went to an IC design house company and be responsible for WiFi products interface and media functions. After providing the convenient interfaces and rich media functions like online radio, spotify and local music play to the client, our products become more attractive to the customers and this increase the chip sale in the market.

    After that, my friend invited me to join their web design team and my main role is to create some new services. We developed a convenient interface to control the surveillance devices and manage the user permissions. I also added a log function to track users' behaviour, integrating the Django default management part with these new functions. This working experience helps me a lot in the web development. After the office time, I usually participate in programming activites to acquire up-to-date technology knowledge and share some programming experience with each other. Through the sharing, the participants can solve problems quicker and more efficiently. Also, I'm building up my skill about deep learning and machine learning online by enrolling in online courses and writing some applications.
